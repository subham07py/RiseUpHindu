Shri Jagannath Ashtakam - Composed by Adi Shankaracharya

Shri Jagannath Ashtakam was composed by Adi Shankaracharya in praise of Lord Jagannath on his visit to Puri. The most important of hymns of Lord Jagannath, the Ashtakam was recited by Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu on his visit to Jagannath temple.

The merit of reciting the sacred Jagannath ashtakam carefully is such that, one becomes sinless and pure hearted and gains entrance to Vishnuloka.
